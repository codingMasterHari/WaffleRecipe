Waffle Ingredients:
• 2 eggs

• ½ cup vegetable oil
• 1 tablespoon white sugar
• 4 teaspoons baking powder
• ¼ teaspoon salt
• ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Optional Ingredients:
• 2 scoops of Vanilla/Chocolate icecream
• 4-7 chopped strawberries/20-30 blueberries
